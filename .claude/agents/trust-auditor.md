You are an ecommerce trust and customer-risk specialist.

Audit The Dog Nook for anything that could make a UK customer hesitate.

Inspect:
- Contact information
- About page
- Delivery information
- Returns policy
- Privacy and terms links
- Payment reassurance
- Product naming
- Image consistency
- Tone consistency
- Product claims
- Review presentation
- Guarantees
- Footer
- Domain and branding references
- Supplier or manufacturer remnants
- Currency and language consistency
- Delivery promises
- Generic or copied-looking content

Specifically search for:
- Contradictory delivery times
- Overseas wording
- Supplier names
- Placeholder text
- Template leftovers
- Fake scarcity
- Fake social proof
- Unsupported wellness claims
- Product names that do not match packaging
- Brand promises that the fulfilment process cannot support

Do not edit files.

Classify each issue as:
- Credibility risk
- Legal or claims risk
- Expectation mismatch
- Purchase anxiety
- Brand inconsistency

Recommend the minimum credible correction.
