# Proposed copy — honest delivery & trust

> The single most important copy change: **one delivery promise, told the same way
> everywhere, that is actually true.** Fill `{…}` with the real fulfilment reality
> (including overseas dispatch if that's the case). Honest-and-longer beats
> fast-but-false — broken delivery promises drive disputes, chargebacks and 1-star
> reviews, and misleading delivery claims are a named unfair practice under UK law.

## Decide the truth first (internal)
Answer these before writing anything customer-facing:
- Where do orders actually ship **from**? {UK stock / CJ overseas / mixed}
- Real **dispatch** time (order → shipped): {e.g. 1–3 working days}
- Real **delivery** time (shipped → doorstep): {e.g. 5–10 working days if overseas}
- Any items genuinely UK-stocked/faster? {list them — only if true}

## Site-wide delivery line (use identically on trust strip, PDP panel, FAQ, checkout)
- If overseas: `Dispatched in {1–3} working days · Delivered in {7–12} working days · Free UK delivery over £35`
- If UK-stocked: `Dispatched in {1–2} working days · Delivered in {3–5} working days · Free UK delivery over £35`

Do **not** mix "same-day UK warehouse" with "delivered 4–7 days" on different pages.
Pick the true one and repeat it.

## Trust-strip cell (replace the current dispatch cell if not verifiably true)
- `🚚 Honest delivery` / `Real times, shown up front` — links to the delivery page.

## FAQ — delivery (replace current)
**Q: How much is delivery and how long does it take?**
`Free UK delivery on orders over £35; under that, the exact cost is shown before you
pay — never a surprise. Orders are dispatched in {X} working days and typically
arrive within {Y} working days. {If relevant: Some items ship from our overseas
supplier, so we build that into the times above and tell you honestly rather than
promise next-day.}`

## FAQ — "Is this a dropshipping store?" (replace evasive answer)
**Q: Is this a dropshipping store?**
`We're a small UK team that curates a tight range for anxious and rescue dogs — we
don't list everything we can find. {Some of our products are made and shipped by our
manufacturing partner {overseas/in X}, which is why we're upfront about delivery
times.} What you get from us: a hand-picked range, honest delivery, a 30-day
money-back guarantee, and a real person who answers your emails — usually the same
day.`
*(This is transparent without being self-sabotaging. Adjust to the true supply
setup. Don't claim "not dropshipping" if the model is dropshipping.)*
