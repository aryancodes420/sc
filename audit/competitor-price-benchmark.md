## What competitors do well (copy these moves)
- **FunnyFuzzy** — rich lifestyle + in-use photography and short video; problem-based collections
  ("calming", "cave beds for anxious dogs"). Their imagery *is* the conversion engine.
- **Lords & Labradors** — a dedicated "Anxious Pet" collection + "Puppy Starter Packs" (bundles);
  premium faux-fur positioning; free delivery threshold (£75).
- **Snuggle Puppy** — sells the *outcome* ("helped 2M dogs settle") and an All-in-One kit; strong
  emotional new-puppy narrative. Their heartbeat toy is £34.99 — your £24.99 undercuts it hard.
- **ThunderShirt** — leans on evidence ("80% see improvement") without medical claims. Safe framing
  to echo on the Calming Coat (comfort/pressure, never "cure").
