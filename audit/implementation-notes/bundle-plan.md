## Naming logic
- The **problem** is in the name (First Days / Fireworks / Home-Alone / Calm).
- The **tier** is the noun: **Kit** = focused starter · **Bundle** = signature · **System** = complete flagship.
- Fireworks is a deliberate **2-tier ladder** (Ready £39.99 → Survival £84.99) — your biggest season, worth catching both wallets.
