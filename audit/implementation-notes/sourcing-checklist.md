## Global sourcing rules (apply to every item)
1. **Platform:** CJdropshipping first (better QC + UK/US warehouses), AliExpress backup, Doba for tech.
2. **Supplier filter:** 4.7★+, 500+ orders, 90-day+ history. Ignore brand-name retail listings.
3. **Neutral packaging = hard gate.** Confirm plain/unbranded box before ordering. Mismatched branding breaks trust + causes refunds.
4. **Ask every supplier:** unit cost · shipping cost + method to UK · delivery window · MOQ (want 1) · is there a **UK/EU warehouse**.
5. **Copy/legal:** comfort/enrichment framing only — "keeps them busy / helps them settle", never "cures anxiety". No medical claims.
6. **Log the confirmed cost + supplier link here** and in `live-catalog-changes.md`.
